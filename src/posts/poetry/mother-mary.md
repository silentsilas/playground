title: Mother Mary (John 2:5)
layout: poetry
---

O, mother Mary,
Is your heart full of regret?

You let him know
The wine's run out,
But his time's not come just yet.

Or, mother Mary,
Is your heart now full of joy?

"He let me know
What will be done,
So servants, obey my boy."
